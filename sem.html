What is a Recovery Phrase?

It is a 12-word phrase that can be used to restore your wallet.

Does Ronin Wallet support Recovery Phrases generated by other wallets?

Yes, Ronin Wallet currently supports all EVM-compatible 12-word Recovery Phrases generated by Metamask, Trust, Coinbase Wallet, etc.

Is it safe to enter it into Ronin Wallet?

Yes. It will be stored locally and never leave your device without your explicit permission.
